Introduction to Geon Theory

Published on 2019-01-14

Geon Theory is a pretty minimal extension of general relativity that purports to derive most of the basic phenomenology of quantum mechanics. Rather than “general relativity from quantum mechanics” we are attempting “QM from GR”. The foundations for this work come from Mark Hadley’s thesis, Quantum Mechanics from General Relativity and the recent research on AdS/CFT and gauge–gravity duality, which Leonard Susskind has characterized by ER=EPR and GR=QM .

Susskind and Maldacena, and colleagues, still think GR emerges from QM. Hadley thought the reverse.

The geon theory acronym would be (GR+CTCs = QM), or even (GR+CTCs$\Rightarrow$QM).

For more of the background natural philosophy behind Geon Theory see the basics of T4G .
